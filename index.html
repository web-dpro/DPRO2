COMPANY HISTORY

PT DIAN PERMATA OMEGA
Since 2014

BUSINESS STARTING


D'PRO Entertainment is a full service event management company established in Jakarta, was founded by Dani Telapary in 2004, and gained solid years of experience in developing and managing several event in the Jabodetabek.

CREATED BY PASSION


D'PRO Entertainment was created by pairing together our dream,ideas,and passion for entertainment business and event. We bring a fresh approach to the event management industry. We treat our client's event with a comprehensive plan and strategic goals to ensure the event is delivered on time and on budget.

CREATIVE & KNOWLEDGE


We offer wide range of experience,creativity & knowledge in event planning services. We can cater to any type of event : big or small ,private or public. Our service include all facets of strategic event planning ,that maybe requested either as a package or ala carte.

TO MEET CLIENT'S OBJECTIVE


Our team of experts has considerable experience in creating and managing themed event designed specifically to meet client's objective within considerable budget. Our creativity and excellent working communicatiins skills have provided us with high client retention rates.

AS OUR VISION


To be the leading company that offers the most creative,exceptional and unforgettable event to the entire Indonesia.
